Welcome to the Great Lakes Exoplanet Area Meeting (GLEAM) 2023!

GLEAM 2023 will be held in the Indiana Memorial Union on the campus of the Indiana University in Bloomington, IN, on October 27-28, 2023.

As we continue the esteemed tradition of regional exoplanet meetings, we're excited to host this year's gathering at the Indiana Memorial Union on the beautiful campus of Indiana University at the end of this year's October. GLEAM has always been a beacon for the Great Lakes region's researchers passionate about exoplanets, planet formation, and related disciplines. Dive into the range of activities we have planned, from engaging lighting talks to our regular talks, ensuring every attendee can make the most of this enriching experience!
